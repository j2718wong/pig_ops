Account Pig Operations Settings

This application only records your data. We do not recommend settings, number of days when to do a pig operation, or brand of vaccines or injectables to your pigs. However,since these pig operations are visible to the staff working in your farm, it is recommended you put a description of what to do and correct dosages in a pig operation to deliver consistent results to your pigs. The default settings are generally what the majority of pig farms are using.

Gestating Sow Operations

This is a list of activities to be performed on a gestating sow in every production cycle.
Every new production entry will create these operations and send alerts to designated people as the number of days from gestation is reached or if not yet done.

Lactating Piglets Operations

This is a list of activities to be performed on a lactating piglets in every production cycle.

Lactating Sow Operations

This is a list of activities to be performed on a lactating sow in every production cycle.
Every new production entry will create these operations and send alerts as the number of days from piglets birth is reached.

Gilts Operations

This is a list of activities to be performed on Gilts before production ready.
