Legal framework: UK GDPR / EU General Data Protection Regulation

  • Right to erasure: "Right to be forgotten"

  • Right to portability: Receive data in machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Anytime, with equal ease as given

Your Rights:

🇳🇿 New Zealand

Legal framework: Privacy Act 2020

Your Rights:

  • Information Privacy Principles (IPPs): We comply with the 13 IPPs
  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Notifiable breaches: We will notify you and the Privacy Commissioner if a serious breach occurs

🇺🇸 United States

Note: Privacy laws vary by state. The following applies based on your state of residence.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know: What personal information we collect, use, disclose
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out: We do not sell your personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination: No retaliation for exercising rights
  • Right to correct: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to limit: Limit use of sensitive personal information

Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA)

  • Right to access
  • Right to delete
  • Right to correct
  • Right to opt-out of targeted advertising (we do not engage in this)

🇵🇭 Philippines

Legal framework: Data Privacy Act 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)

Your Rights:

  • Right to be informed: How your data is processed
  • Right to access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to correction: Dispute inaccuracy and request correction
  • Right to erasure: Suspend, withdraw, or remove personal information
  • Right to object: Opt out of processing
  • Right to data portability: Receive data in electronic format
  • Right to complain: File with National Privacy Commission

Cross-border transfer: Your data may be transferred to Singapore; this is permitted under NPC Circular 16-03.

BSP compliance: For GCash/Maya payments, we follow BSP Circular 982 regulations.
