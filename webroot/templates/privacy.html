🐷 SuperPig Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026 · JSysDev
Your privacy matters. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, apps, or services.

1. Who we are

This privacy policy applies to JSysDev (referred to as “we”, “us”, or “our”). We provide data management and data analytics for pig farmers.

2. What information we collect

2.1 Information you give us

When you register, purchase, or use our service, you may provide:

  • Account information: name, email address, password, farm location, country.
  • Farm data: information about your breeding stocks, production records (this is your data – you own it).
  • Communications: when you contact support or send feedback.

2.2 Information we collect automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device type, operating system.
  • Pages you visit, time spent, and how you interact with the service.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies.

2.3 Information from third parties

If you use social login (Facebook, Google, TikTok), we receive basic profile information (name, email) from those services.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and maintain the service: create your account and give you access to features.
  • To improve and personalize: understand how users interact with the app, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To communicate with you: send service updates, support messages, and (with your consent) marketing offers.
  • To comply with law: meet legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. When we share your information

  • With service providers: companies that help us run the business (cloud hosting, email delivery, customer support).
  • For legal reasons: if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.
  • With your consent: when you explicitly agree.

5. Data security and retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide the service.

6. Cookies & similar technologies

We use cookies and local storage to keep you logged in, understand usage, and remember your preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your rights & choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, and portability of your data. To exercise any right, email jsysdev.contact@gmail.com.
